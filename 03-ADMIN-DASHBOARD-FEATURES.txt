--- ADD/EDIT PRODUCT FORM ---

Route: /admin/products/new or /admin/products/[id]/edit

Form Fields:

Product Information:
- Product Name* (text)
  Placeholder: "e.g., STEMCARE Facial Treatment"

- Description* (rich text editor or textarea)
  Placeholder: "Detailed description of the treatment..."
  Minimum 50 characters

Pricing & Quotas:
- Price (MYR)* (number)
  Format: RM XXX.XX
  Minimum: RM 1.00

- Deposit Percentage* (number, 0-100)
  Default: 30
  Help text: "Percentage required for deposit payments (0 = full payment only)"

Service Details:
- Duration (minutes)* (number)
  Dropdown or input: 15, 30, 45, 60, 90, 120, 180, Custom
  Minimum: 15 minutes

- Daily Quota* (number)
  Help text: "Maximum appointments per day for this service"
  Minimum: 1
  Default: 5

Status:
- Active (toggle switch)
  Default: ON
  Help text: "Inactive products won't appear in patient booking"

[Save Product] [Cancel]

Validation:
- All required fields must be filled
- Price must be positive number
- Deposit percentage 0-100
- Duration minimum 15 minutes
- Quota minimum 1

On Save:
1. Create/Update Product record
2. Show success message
3. Redirect to /admin/products

--- ARCHIVE PRODUCT ---

 Existing appointments will not be affected.
 
 You can reactivate this product anytime from the Archived Products section."

On Confirm:
1. Set Product.isActive = false
2. Remove from active product list
3. Move to archived section
